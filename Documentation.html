  • Writing Effective AI Instructions

    Your AI assistant is only as good as the instructions you provide. Well-crafted prompts are the key to successful campaigns and satisfied customers.

    üéØ Essential Elements of Great AI Instructions

    • Clear Objective: Define exactly what the AI should accomplish
    • Company Identity: Establish tone, personality, and brand voice
    • Conversation Flow: Guide how the AI should structure the call
    • Boundary Setting: Specify what the AI should and shouldn't do
    1. Inbound Call Instructions: Configure how AI handles incoming calls
      • Professional Greeting: "You are a professional receptionist for [Company Name]. Always greet callers warmly and identify yourself."
      • Information Gathering: "Ask for caller's name, company, and reason for calling. Take detailed messages for unavailable staff."
      • Knowledge Base Usage: "Use the company knowledge base to answer frequently asked questions about services, pricing, and policies."
      • Escalation Rules: "For complex technical issues or urgent matters, offer to transfer to appropriate department or take detailed callback information."
    2. Outbound Campaign Instructions: Customize AI behavior for cold calling
      • Opening Hook: "Introduce yourself and company, then immediately provide value or ask permission to continue."
      • Pain Point Discovery: "Ask open-ended questions to understand their current challenges with [your solution area]."
      • Value Proposition: "Clearly explain how your solution addresses their specific pain points."
      • Call to Action: "Guide conversation toward scheduling a demo, consultation, or next meeting."
      • Objection Handling: "If they say 'not interested,' ask one clarifying question to understand why before gracefully ending."
    3. Sample Templates:
      Inbound Support Template:

      "You are Sarah, a friendly and professional customer service representative for TechCorp Solutions. Your role is to provide excellent customer support, answer questions about our software products, and help customers resolve issues. Always be patient, empathetic, and solution-focused. If you don't know an answer, admit it and offer to connect them with someone who can help. Use the knowledge base to provide accurate information about our products, pricing, and policies. For technical issues beyond basic troubleshooting, offer to schedule a callback with our technical team."

      Sales Campaign Template:

      "You are Alex, a consultative sales representative for MarketingPro Agency. You're calling small business owners to help them improve their digital marketing ROI. Start by asking about their current marketing challenges - don't pitch immediately. Listen to their specific pain points, then explain how our marketing automation platform has helped similar businesses increase leads by 40% while reducing costs. Your goal is to schedule a 15-minute demo, not to close a sale on this call. If they express interest, use the scheduling link to book a demo. If they're not ready, ask permission to follow up in 3 months and send helpful resources."

    4. Advanced Instruction Techniques:
      • Persona Development: Give your AI a name and personality for more natural conversations
      • Scenario Planning: Include instructions for common situations and objections
      • Compliance Guidelines: Include industry-specific regulations and do-not-call considerations
      • Data Collection: Specify exactly what information to gather and how to store it
      • Follow-up Actions: Define when and how AI should trigger next steps or alerts

  • Troubleshooting

    1. Issue: The module is not activating.
      Try re-uploading the module zip file and ensure you are using the latest version of Perfex CRM.
    2. Issue: Campaigns are not processing calls.
      Check that your AI provider API keys are valid and phone numbers are properly configured. Ensure the campaign status is set to "Running".
    3. Issue: Knowledge base files are not uploading.
      Verify that your files are under 300KB and in a supported format. Check your server's file upload limits and permissions.
    4. Issue: AI calls are failing with API errors.
      Verify your API keys for Vapi.ai or Bland.ai are correct and active. Check that your phone numbers are properly configured and your AI provider account has sufficient balance.
    5. Issue: Call logs are not showing transcripts.
      Transcripts may take a few minutes to process after call completion. Ensure your AI provider account has transcription services enabled.
    6. Issue: Manual calls are not working from lead profiles.
      Ensure the lead has a valid phone number and that your default AI provider settings are properly configured.
    7. Issue: Campaign CSV upload fails.
      Check that your CSV file follows the required format with proper column headers. Ensure phone numbers are in valid format (preferably E.164).
